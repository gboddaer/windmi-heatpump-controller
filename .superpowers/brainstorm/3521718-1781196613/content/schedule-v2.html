Schedule — Simplified

One question at a time. How should the schedule be structured?

A

Time ranges

Define blocks like "Mon-Fri 6:00-9:00 Heating 40°C". Each range has a start time, end time, mode, and temperature. Simple list, easy to read on any screen.

B

Slot grid (scrollable)

48 slots per day in a compact vertical list. Tap a slot to change it. Scrollable on mobile, full width on desktop. Day group selector at the top.

C

One schedule, repeat

Single list of time ranges that applies to all days. No day groups, no per-day override. One schedule, done.
